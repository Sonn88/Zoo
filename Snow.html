The snow exhibit is a cold and snowy place for the animals who live here .

In the snow exhibit we have many types of animal We will show you the most popular in legal zoo and tell you some fun facts.

These two cute things are the two harp seals we have in legal zoo. Their names are Molly and Skyer they are harp seals. During mating season, female harp seals form large colonies on floating ice and give birth to young. Older seals return annually to pack ice to molt. They lose their pelts and top layers of skin and grow a new fur coat every year. Harp seals are sometimes called saddleback seals because of the dark, saddlelike marking on the back and sides of their light yellow or gray bodies of the adults.

Though Molly and Skyer are so aborable we have many other cute animals in the snow exhibit Like Ace here He is an amazing artic fox.

Two super cool facts about artic foxes fur changes seasonally ,in the winter they have fluffy white coats that blend into the snow and in the summer they shed these coats for brownish or grayish also shorter fur that helps them camouflage into bare rocks and dirt. Arctic fox also are opportunistic omnivores and very curious.
